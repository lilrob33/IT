YEILD TO THE EVOLUTION

PCB Fabrication





At Integrated Test, we have a strong track record of investing in the latest tools and processes to ensure that we can fabricate your test boards with very competitive prices and lead-times.

For starters, we utilize the Genesis 2000 pre-production CAM system. Genesis 2000 allows us to combine planning, product engineering and tooling into a single process, and equally important, into a single database.

The tool also features enhanced DFM capabilities to dramatically reduce shipping delays due to yield problems on complex builds.

On the manufacturing floor, we continue to upgrade tools targeting leading-edge capabilities as well as process time reductions to ensure that we keep pace with the rapidly advancing technology in ATE test boards.
